ABOUT US

I AM YOUNG AND IMPACTING started as a project built in November 2021 with headquaters in Antananarivo, Madagascar and inspires to be extended to other African countries. Be part of our special project let's make a change. Sign up HERE

OUR MISSION

1. Encourage youths to develop innovative projects in different domains of life.
2. Teach youths the importance of time management and believing in their dreams.
3. Give the youths a platform to express themselves
4. Adress the population about the importance of listening to the voice of the youths

OUR VISION

Youths are the foundation of the leadership of tomorrow, but most of the time they are absent from the building process of our communities/Nations, are ignorant of their rights and do not believe in their dreams. IAYI aims at making youths our partners of today through our conferences-festivals, panel talks, and sensibilization campaigns so as to ensure quality leadership for tomorrow.
